- Stomp Balloon: The figure jumps up and stomps down onto a low balloon resting near the concrete; it bursts flat under the boot with a loud pop and a puff of air, pale shreds scattering across the wet ground — the burst balloon is gone, only flat shreds left on the ground.
- Spin-Kick Combo: The figure spins into a wide roundhouse kick, sweeping a leg through a whole cluster of white balloons; they BURST one after another in a rapid chain of pops, torn pale membranes fluttering down all across the grey courtyard — every balloon caught in the sweep is gone, leaving clear air across the courtyard.
- Jump (Space): The figure springs upward off both feet, leaping high off the wet concrete up toward the drifting balloons, boots lifting clear of the ground before dropping back down and landing in a low crouch.
- Crouch (C held): The camera lowers as the figure crouches down low over the wet concrete, ducking beneath the bobbing balloons into a compact stance; the viewpoint sinks smoothly to a low, near-ground vantage and settles there.
- Stand (C release): The figure straightens back up out of the crouch, rising to full standing height as the camera lifts smoothly back to its normal eye-level vantage among the pale balloons.
